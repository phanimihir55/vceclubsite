THE swayam ED- cell

Swayam The Entrepreneurship Cell of Vasavi College of Engineering is a manifestation of the efforts, ideas, dreams and aspirations of not one but all who are associated with it. It is established to develop and nourish the latent entrepreneurial spirit which is inherent in every student.

Toastmasters club

Since 1924, Toastmasters International has been recognized as the leading organization dedicated to communication and leadership skill development!!Aims to enhance spoken skills.

MUN - VCE chapter

The MUN club is popular mainly because it involves and teaches participants researching, public speaking, debating, and writing skills, in addition to honing their critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership abilities.
